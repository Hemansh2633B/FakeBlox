Nether wastes – The common Nether look: netherrack, glowstone, lava lakes, ghasts, piglins, and magma cubes.
Soul sand valley – Packed with soul sand and soul soil; movement is slower without Soul Speed.
Basalt deltas – Gray volcanic-looking biome rich in basalt and blackstone; bastion remnants do not generate here.
Crimson forest – Dense crimson fungus biome with piglins and hoglins, plus weeping vines and shroomlights.
Warped forest – Blue fungal biome with warped nylium/roots/sprouts and mostly endermen + striders.
